Insight of the Week
Why Lenskart has a 67% gross margin while Nykaa has 28% — in the same "retail" category
Both are D2C retail companies selling consumer products. But their cost structures are completely different because of one decision: Lenskart manufactures its own lenses. Own manufacturing = 60–70% margins. Reselling others' products = 15–28% margins. Same sector, completely different P&L.
