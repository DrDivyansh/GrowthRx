Key Takeaways
Nykaa
The platform play with an own-brand ace up its sleeve
→Currently a low-margin reseller business — but own brands (Dot & Key, Kay Beauty) at 50–60% margins could change everything
→Marketing efficiency is the real edge — 75% repeat rate and brand content keeps CAC low vs pure performance-marketing competitors
→B2B Superstore growing 84% YoY — a completely separate revenue stream that analysts are undervaluing
Lenskart
A manufacturing business disguised as a D2C retailer
→67% gross margin from own lens manufacturing — a moat built over 10+ years that competitors can't quickly replicate
→International (42% revenue) is the margin expansion story — Japan ASP is 5–10x India's ASP with same manufacturing cost
→Depreciation cliff was always going to reverse — FY25 proved it with ₹297 Cr PAT. The 'loss-making' narrative was misleading
